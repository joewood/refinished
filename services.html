Services
I offer a wide variety of services including, drop-ins, walking and pet-sitting. I understand that every pet is unique, so I tailor my services to meet your pet's individual needs.
DOG WALKING

Daily walking is important for both the physical and mental well-being of your pet. Getting the exercise and stimulation they need to keep them happy and healthy. The cost of these services is:

  1. 20-minute walks or drop-in: $20
  2. 30-minute walks or drop-in: $30
  3. Extra dogs (more than 2): $5 per dog.

These visits may include: a walk, potty break, feeding and medications depending on your requirements.

Please note that walks provided on Holidays will be an extra $10 per walk.

Holidays include: New Year's Eve, New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day.

Pet Sitting

Dog Sitting

Going away on vacation can be stressful for both your pet and the family. I will come and stay in your home with your pet(s) overnight. This may include daily walks, feeding, play, medication, etc. Your pet(s) will receive the care in the comfort of their own home. The cost of this service is:

  1. $65 per night

Holiday pet sitting will be an extra $20 per night.

Holidays include: New Year's Eve, New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day.

If required, I will collect mail/newspapers take out garbage, and/or water plants.

Cats and Small Pets Visits

This service is for cats, birds, rabbits, chickens, etc. that need to be looked after while you’re away but may not need an overnight stay. The cost of this service is:

  1. $20 per night

These visits may include feeding, fresh water, cage or litter box cleaning and medications if necessar

Holiday pet sitting will be an extra $10 per visit.

Holidays include: New Year's Eve, New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day.

If required, I will collect mail/newspapers, take out garbage, and/or water plants.

Vet Visits

Your pet’s health is very important, and you may not have the time to take them for their check ups or vaccinations. Walk With Wagz will take, wait with your pet and then return them to your home.

  1. $35 per hour.

Vet must be in local area (within 20 miles).
