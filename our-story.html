Our Story

I started Walk With Wags because of my love for dogs and a desire to provide a reliable dog/pet walking/sitting service for busy pet owners in Rockland County.

I believe that every dog deserves to live a happy and healthy life. I strive to provide a fun and safe environment for your pet, while also giving them the experience and attention they need to thrive.

Using Walk With Wags, you can be sure your pets’ needs will be well taken care of, when you’re not around.

Walk With Wags is licensed and insured.
